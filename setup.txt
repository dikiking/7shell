freebsd9.0 apache2.41+ mysql5.5.21+ php5.4+memcached 一键安装
设置163镜像 终端执行以下命令
setenv PACKAGESITE http://mirrors.163.com/FreeBSD/ports/i386/packages-9-current/Latest/
fetch http://7shell.googlecode.com/svn/fastbsd
chmod +x fastbsd
./fastbsd


启动apache
service apache start 
启动mysql
service mysql-server start
